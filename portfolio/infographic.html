Infographic

This infographic was made in my Elements 2 class. I did 3 different workups of the infographic, where this one was the final product. I chose the topic of cats being the largest cause of endangerment of native species because I myself have a bird and don't particularly like cats. When I found this information, I thought it made sense to raise awareness of the danger of outdoor and feral cats to the native species of various animals of North America, and I still stick to this particular stance today.
