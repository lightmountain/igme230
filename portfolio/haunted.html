Haunted House Poster

This poster was for an ad of a local haunted house called Haunted Hayrides of Greater Rochester. I wanted to convey the horror someone might feel when going there, so I wanted to make sure this was extra gory. I made it by compiling a few different faces, including that of my own, to create this screaming person who's mind is literally blown.
