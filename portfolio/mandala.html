Mandala Animaiton

In my animation class, our first project was to design and animate a mandala that represented us in some way. I decided to take inspiration from my Nicaraguan heritage and use things that I think of when I think of Nicaragua. Some of these things are rice and beans, Lake Nicaragua, and how hot it gets there. The music is from a song called "La Palomita Guasiruca," which translated literally into English means "the little dove who is dove-like. I find that really funny.
