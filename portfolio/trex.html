T-Rex Animation

This animation was part of a team project where we were prompted to make our own snippet of an animation and then run it all together. Our theme was "The Last Day," as is made clear by the dinosaurs and asteroid that gets closer and closer in each snippet. I chose a T-Rex eating because I wanted the dinosaur to be doing something it would normally do in its habitat.
